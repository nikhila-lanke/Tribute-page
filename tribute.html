TRIBUTE TO DR. APJ ABDUL KALAM


The Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, an eminent Indian scientist and statesman, was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Serving as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007, he was widely known as the "People's President" for his connect with the masses. With a background in aerospace engineering, Dr. Kalam made significant contributions to India's space and missile programs, earning him the moniker "Missile Man of India."
